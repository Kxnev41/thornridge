About Thornridge

Thornridge is a living, breathing modded world where survival meets storytelling. Explore vast lands, discover forgotten ruins, and craft your own destiny among friends and rivals. Every player shapes the future of Thornridge.

Lore

Long ago, the valley of Thornridge thrived under the guidance of the ancient builders. Now, as magic reawakens and factions rise, adventurers seek to claim the ruins and uncover what lies beneath the soil.

Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No griefing or stealing.
  • Stay in character during roleplay events.
  • No cheating, x-ray, or exploits.
  • Have fun and build the story together!
