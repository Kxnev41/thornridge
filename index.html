About Thornridge

Thornridge is a living modded world where survival meets storytelling. Explore the city, uncover ruins, and shape your destiny among friends and rivals.

Lore

Long ago, Thornridge thrived under the guidance of the ancient builders. Now, as magic reawakens, adventurers explore to claim the ruins and uncover what lies beneath.

Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No griefing or stealing.
  • Stay in character during RP events.
  • No cheats or exploits.
  • Have fun and build the story together!
