About Thornridge

Thornridge is a living modded world where survival meets storytelling. Explore our city, discover forgotten ruins, and craft your own destiny among friends and rivals. Every player shapes the future of Thornridge.

Lore

Long ago, Thornridge thrived under the guidance of the ancient builders. Now, as magic reawakens and rises, adventurers explore to claim the ruins and uncover what lies beneath the dirt.

Rules

  • Respect all players and staff.
  • No griefing or stealing.
  • Stay in character during roleplay events.
  • No cheating, x-ray, or exploits.
  • Have fun and build the story together!

Required Mods

To join Thornridge, you’ll need to install the following mods (Forge 1.20.1 recommended):

  • Security Craft
  • Architectury API
  • Cloth Config
  • Coroutil (Forge)
  • Create
  • Create Railways Navigator
  • Crops Need Sky
  • Farmer’s Delight
  • Framework (MrCrayfish)
  • MCEF
  • MrCrayfish’s Furniture Refurbished
  • SkinsRestorer (Forge, Lionarius)
  • Thirst Was Taken
  • WATUT
  • WebDisplays
  • Macaw’s Bridges
  • Macaw’s Doors
  • Macaw’s Windows
  • Macaw’s Fences and Walls
  • Macaw’s Trapdoors
  • Macaw’s Roofs
  • Macaw’s Furniture
  • Macaw’s Lights and Lamps
  • Macaw’s Path and Pavings
  • Macaw’s Paintings
  • Macaw’s Stairs
  • City Modernities (Port/Renewed)
  • Nickname Easy
  • Simple Voice Chat

⚠️ Warning: Be careful not to duplicate mods!
