About Thornridge

Thornridge is a living modded world where survival meets storytelling. Explore the city, uncover ruins, and shape your destiny among friends and rivals.

Lore

Thornridge, nestled between small mountains and rivers, isn't the biggest city in ,,Minecraftia" - but atleast it exists, right? Built from old stone, wood, and tradition, it's a mix of modern progress and countryside peace. Founded a few generations ago by Bistrica Miteva as a mining and trading village, Thornridge slowly grew into the administrative and cultural capital of ,,Minecraftia". It's where decisions are made and stories begin. Thornridge is cozy, historic, and alive with stories. No towering skyscrapers which make you anxious when they're gonna fall, just well-built town halls, houses and gardens full of life. At sunrise, the streets glow soft pink from the cherry trees. At night, lanterns flicker across all of the streets, roads and boulevards. Locals are proud - every brick and blossom tells part of their city's story. Notable places - Central Plaza - Ivanev Memorial Museum "Not vast, but lasting."
